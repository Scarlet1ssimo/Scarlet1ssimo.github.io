Bio

I am a second year MCS master student at University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign, doing research in Illinois LLVM Compiler Research Lab, advised by Prof. Vikram Adve.

My research interest lies in compiler optimizations, automated optimized code generation, performance engineering, and program synthesis. I have a strong background in algorithms and data structures. I look forward to working on questions that address real-world needs.
